The Best Movies According to Perry

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Saving Private Ryan

"Saving Private Ryan" is one of the greatest movies of all time because it masterfully combines harrowing realism, profound storytelling, and deeply human themes to honor the sacrifices of soldiers during World War II

Harry Potter Series

Harry Potter is one of the greatest series of all time because it weaves an enchanting tale of friendship, bravery, and the triumph of good over evil in a richly imagined magical world that has captivated generations.

Avengers: Endgame

Avengers: Endgame is one of the greatest movies of all time because it delivers an epic culmination of over a decade of storytelling, blending emotional depth, thrilling action, and a satisfying resolution that celebrates its beloved characters.
